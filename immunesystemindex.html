Immune System

The Immune System is the best defense system in the human body that protects against disease.

An Immune System detect any unknown agents that wants to harm the body, known as Pathogens (from viruses)

to parasitic worms that recognize them from the organism's healthy tissue. It is compose in two major subsystems

of the immune system: The Innate Immune System (we born with this) and the Adaptive Immune System (vaccination).
