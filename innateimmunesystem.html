Innate Immune System

It is found in vertebrates. It have been said that the Innate Immune System

is a long time evolutionary defense strategy, and is the dominant Immune System

response found in plants, fungus, and insects.

The Major Functions of The Vertebrate Innate Immune System include:

  • Cytokines are specialized chemical mediators by recruiting immune cells to sites of infection.
  • Identify bacteria, activate cells, and discard antibody complexes.
  • Removed unfamiliar substances in organs, tissues, blood and Lymph.
  • Activates through a process known as Antigen presentation (a vital immune process for T-cell immune response).
  • It acts as a physical and chemical barrier to infectious agents.
