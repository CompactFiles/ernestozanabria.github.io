UEFA CHAMPIONS LEAGUE

The UEFA Champions League (also known as the European Cup) is an annual club football competition organised

by the Union of European Football Associations (UERA) and contested by top-division European clubs, deciding

the best team in Europe. It is one of the most prestigious club competition in European football, played by the national

league champions. Introduced in 1955 as the European Champions Clubs' Cup, it was initially a straight knockout

tournament open only to the championship. The competition took on its current name in 1992. adding a round-robin

group stage and allowing multiple multiple entrants from certain countries. This is the biggest tournament that I

love to watch UEFA when I have time most of the time my favorite team plays in that tournament Real Madrid that's

the name of my favorite team.
