Inflammation

It is the first to react of the Immune System to infections or irritations.

Inflammation is stimulated by chemical factors released by injured cells that

stablish a physical barrier against the spread of infection that support healing of

a damaged tissue for a better recovery.

The inflammatory response is characterized by the following symptons:

  • Erythema is redness of the skin, that occurs of the skin injury, infection or inflammation.
  • A warm feeling arround a localized area infected.
  • Swelling of affected tissue,such as throat during common cold or joints affected.
  • Mucus or symptoms like a running nose or cough.
  • Painful joints or a sore throat, or body aches.
  • Dysfunction of the organs or tissues involved.
